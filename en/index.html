We are made for innovative quality services.

MIQS was founded in September of 2015. We’re driven by a passion to provide innovative quality IT consulting services for our global customers. Engineering is in our DNA.

MIQS’ founding team possesses more than a combined four decades of IT industry experience in the US, Hong Kong, and mainland China; across the board, we boast a track record of delivering successfully from initial requirement gathering to final production rollout.

The dedicated staffs of MIQS are well versed in designing and programming real-world solutions with a diverse set of operating systems and programming languages.

Since MIQS was founded, we’ve grown into a company, with staffs in Shenzhen and Chicago offices delivering low latency financial market trading applications, web applications, and mobile applications.
